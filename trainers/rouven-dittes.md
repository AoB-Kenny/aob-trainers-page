## Intro

Rouven comes to this from long meditation retreats, years in therapy and self-experience settings, and shamanic work in the Amazon. He calls what he does integrative process facilitation, built on conscious connected breathing, and the direction is always the same: out of the head and into the body, where the patterns are actually held. He works with people who are stuck, tense, cut off from what they feel, or looking for something that has not arrived yet.

## What he offers

- 1:1 sessions
- Group breathwork circles
- Breathwork training programmes
