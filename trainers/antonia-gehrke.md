## Intro

Antonia works from the premise that what happened to you is not filed away in your thinking. It is held in the body, the nervous system, down to the cellular level, which is why talking about it only gets you so far. She combines breathwork with coaching in 1:1 work, holds group events in Hannover, and runs her own training programmes including *Roots into Soul*.

## What she offers

- 1:1 breathwork and coaching sessions
- Group events
- Training programmes, including *Roots into Soul* and breathwork certification
- Recurring 90-minute *The Art of Breath* workshops
