## Intro

Jana is a clinical psychologist with a master's in psychotherapy and psychoanalysis, licensed as a Heilpraktikerin for psychotherapy and in training for depth-psychological psychotherapy. She certified as a breathwork facilitator in 2025 and uses connected breathing inside a therapeutic frame, alongside psychedelic integration work. Safety, integrity and collaboration are the words she leads with. She works in German and English.

## What she offers

- Depth-oriented psychotherapy for depression, anxiety, relationship and existential concerns
- Breathwork as body-oriented therapeutic work
- Psychedelic integration coaching
- Short-term online sessions
