## Intro

Emi found breathwork on a retreat while recovering from an eating disorder, and what she found was that the eating was never the point. It was covering something that had not been felt. She trained in trauma-sensitive breathwork to Art of Breath standards under Daniel Alan and now works with people on integration rather than optimisation. She is direct about who the work is not for, and she starts everyone with a free first conversation.

## What she offers

- Free first conversation
- Breathwork circles in small groups
- 1:1 long-term coaching
- Workshops, including *Mutterlinien* on maternal bonds and imprinting
- Two-day retreat
