## Intro

Caroline describes her work in three words: grounded, trauma-sensitive, present. She came to breathwork through yoga, trained with Daniel Alan and is licensed through Art of Breath. Her own five-month facilitator training spends its fourteen in-person days on self-regulation and co-regulation, the nervous system, and how to actually hold a process, not just run a protocol. She teaches yoga alongside it, in person and online.

## What she offers

- Breathwork facilitator training, five months, 14 in-person days
- 1:1 Conscious Connected Breathing sessions
- Group sessions and Atemreisen
- Breathing technique workshops for beginners and advanced
- Hatha, Yin and breath-focused yoga, in person and online
