## Intro

Aris has been holding transformational spaces for over fifteen years, and he brings more than breath to them. Twenty-five years as a DJ taught him what music does to a room. He works with breath, movement and sound together, and runs plant medicine retreats in the Amazon each year. His breathwork is gentle in tone and deep in reach. He also co-mentors on Art of Breath trainings.

## What he offers

- 1:1 breathwork sessions
- Group workshops and breathwork seminars
- Ecstatic dance events
- Plant wisdom work, including annual Amazon retreats
- Co-mentoring on Art of Breath trainings
