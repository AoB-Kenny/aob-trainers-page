## Intro

Sandra works across the widest range of anyone on this list. Breathwork facilitator through Art of Breath, yoga teacher, NLP trainer, CQM master coach, sound healing and meditation trainer, plus aerial yoga, Pilates and cacao ceremonies. Her line is that real change starts where you are willing to take responsibility for yourself, out of self-love rather than pressure. She runs retreats in Croatia and Austria and coaches on business as well as personal ground.

## What she offers

- Breathwork circles, group sessions and breathwork training

- Yoga in several styles, including aerial and FeetUp
- NLP training, CQM, systemic constellations
- Sound healing, meditation, cacao ceremonies, Akasha readings, energy work
- Retreats in Croatia and Austria
