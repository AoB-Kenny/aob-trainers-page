## Intro

Martin spent years as a manager in corporate leadership development, founded a startup, and worked at large consultancies. He was performing well and living in reaction mode, and that gap is what started his own work. He now coaches leaders and teams on the move from head to heart, using breathwork for nervous system regulation and shadow work alongside conventional coaching. His client list runs to Rhenus, KfW, DB Schenker and TÜV.

## What he offers

- 1:1 leadership coaching
- Breathwork sessions
- Team development and leadership journeys for executive teams
- In-house corporate programmes
