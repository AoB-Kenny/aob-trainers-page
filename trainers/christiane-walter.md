## Intro

Christiane spent twenty years as a midwife, and she has been accompanying people through hard passages for close to thirty. She sees a breathwork session the way she sees a birth: a whole-person process where she can hold, steady and point the way, but you walk it yourself. Her workshop series *Mutterlinien* brings attachment theory, nervous system regulation and generational patterning into that work. Facilitator training starts January 2027.

## What she offers

- 1:1 breathwork sessions
- *Mutterlinien* workshop series on maternal imprinting, attachment and freedom, max 12 women per group
- SummerBreathe group sessions
- Breathwork facilitator training, first cohort starts 22 January 2027 (places already going)
