## Intro

Hanna spent twenty years working with young people, parents and refugees in pedagogy and psychology, long enough to know that insight alone does not change anyone. Real change, as she puts it, does not happen in the head. She is a certified Art of Breath instructor and a Somatic Release practitioner through the Rubin Institut, and she builds her work in stages: circles first, then a three-month mentoring, then 1:1 if you want to go further.

## What she offers

- Breathwork circles for beginners and experienced practitioners
- Three-month mentoring programme, weekly videos plus monthly group sessions (requires 3 to 4 circles first)
- Intensive 1:1 coaching combining breathwork and coaching
- Somatic release work
