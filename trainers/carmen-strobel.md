## Intro

Carmen trains coaches and therapists to facilitate breathwork, and she is clear that you cannot take someone further than you have gone yourself. Her programme is built on deep personal work first, technique second, with trauma-sensitive practice running through all of it. Alongside the training she teaches yoga and meditation and runs retreats. She describes herself as a nomad, at home in herself rather than in a place.

## What she offers

- Breathwork facilitator training for coaches, therapists and individuals
- 1:1 sessions and group programmes
- Yoga (500-hour Hatha certification) and meditation
- Energy massage and abdominal chi massage
- Retreats in changing locations
- Corporate and professional coaching applications
